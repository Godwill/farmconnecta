About FarmConnecta


About Us

FarmConnecta is a livestock Information System that provides farmers with marketing information and Matimela (Stray Livestock) knowledge base system. The main goal of Farmconnecta is to assists farmers in rural Botswana to be able to access and maximize the benefits from using technology to make informed marketing decisions and identify appropriate markets for their livestock and also be able to search for their livestock from in Matimela knowledge base system.

At Your Service

Livestock Marketing Information

Farmconnecta provides transparent, timely and realiable livestock marketing information. It gives access to information about livestock prices at different markets, hence farmers are able to identify markets which offers best prices for their livestock.

Livestock Marketing Notifications

Farmconnecta has a web application which posts SMSs from farmers as they market their livestock to potential markets

Matimela (Stray Livestock)

Matimela is defined as any stray livestock. This animals are collected and registered at Matimela kraal. Farmconnecta helps owners of this livestock kept in Matimela kraals by providing them with an SMS based platform to query the matimela database for their livestock in a timely and efficient manner.
